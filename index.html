About Elena Rivera

I'm a creative website designer based in San Francisco, California. With a background in graphic design and front-end development, I bridge the gap between aesthetics and functionality. My mission is to craft digital experiences that not only look stunning but also deliver exceptional user experiences.

Over the past 6 years, I've collaborated with startups, agencies, and established brands to create websites that tell compelling stories. I believe that great design is invisible — it guides users naturally and makes complex tasks feel simple.

When I'm not designing, you can find me exploring nature, contributing to open-source projects, or mentoring aspiring designers.
